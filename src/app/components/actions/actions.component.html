Correspondence

This work started with a letter that Doug Lagarde sent to the Severn Community on June 3, 2020. Catalyzed by Headmaster Lagarde's evasiveness on issues of racism in that letter, many of us sent emails to the school calling not just for a better response to the current moment, but also for serious reflection on numerous reports of racist indicents at the school, and for a plan to develop a true anti-racist culture at Severn.
